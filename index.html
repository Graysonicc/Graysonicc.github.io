About Me

Selected Publications

Segment-Aligned Policy Optimization (SAPO)

ICML 2026

SAPO formulates reasoning as a step-wise Markov Decision Process and performs policy optimization over coherent reasoning segments, leading to more accurate credit assignment, smoother optimization, and improved reasoning performance.

Code: https://github.com/Graysonicc/SAPO

EgoExoLLM

Submitted to IEEE TCSVT

A dual-memory multimodal framework for efficient cross-view video understanding. EgoExoLLM introduces a Global-Action Memory Bank and a Global-to-Action Projector for long-video reasoning.

MG-ReID

Submitted to IEEE TMM

A multi-granularity text-to-image person re-identification framework with adaptive feature alignment and verb-aware semantic reconstruction.
